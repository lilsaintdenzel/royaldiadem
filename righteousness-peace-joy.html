righteousness, peace, and joy:
the foundation of His throne.
The Kingdom progression.

"For the kingdom of God is not a matter of eating and drinking but of righteousness and peace and joy in the Holy Spirit." — Romans 14:17

The foundation of His throne is righteousness. And because of this righteousness, this right standing with God, we experience peace.

Not the peace the world gives—temporary, conditional, fragile. But the peace that surpasses all understanding. The peace that holds steady when circumstances rage. The peace that comes from knowing you are reconciled to the Father.

From this peace, which surpasses all understanding, joy overflows.

Joy is simply the expression of inner peace. It's not dependent on what's happening around you. It's the result of what's settled within you.

Righteousness establishes your position. Peace guards your heart. Joy radiates from your spirit. This is the Kingdom sequence. This is how His throne operates—and it's the same foundation He's building in you.
